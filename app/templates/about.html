About Metamundo

I wanted to create a project that in its principles is simple, but has the potential to deliver some unexpected, funny, and interesting results. With the "give tools, see what happens" idea, I intend to write some simple rules about 'blobs' in this game, and see what people come up with.

As of the 23th of June, 2016, there are not a lot of dynamics yet. But ho and lo! More is coming up! I ensure that whatever happens, the 'live' version, does not throw any bugs, so bear with me while I add new features.

Changelog

v1.1 04-07-2016

Welcome back! I've been working on JavaScript & HTML5 Canvas foolishness to see if I have a player walk around in the world, with a nice background and such. It's still very simple, but having the background images prerender before they come into view correctly, actually wasn't all that simple. Probably there's a kind of JS library for it somewhere, but where's the fun in that when we're learning the flexibility of the language.

Besides that, there are a couple of new, small changes live:

  • The state (on/off) of the blob population control now persists through new pages.
  • Not entering numbers for coordinates now tell you what's what. That's right.
  • Tiny layout changes

So either way, stay tuned for the 'walking around in the blobworld' shenanigans. Also - do you still not know what blobs are? Well ...
